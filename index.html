How to Buy

1. Create a Wallet

download metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

2. Get Some $Base

Have BaseETH in your wallet to switch to $Lofi. If you don’t have any BaseETH, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

3. Connect to Apestore

connect to ape.store. Go to ape.store in google chrome or on the browser. Connect your wallet. Paste the $LOFI token address into ape.store, select Lofi, and confirm. When your wallet prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

4. Swap $BaseETH for Lofi

Switch base ETH for $Lofi. We have ZERO taxes so you don’t need to worry about buying with a specific slippage, although you may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
